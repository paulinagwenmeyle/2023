Global warming influences soil and the natural life cycle. Irregular and broken structures mirror cracking in the ground. 
Distortion, Where Weill it lead us? We are nomads in a drought on a promising journey to a refreshing oasis. A place where we are driven by curiosity to explore and to forget. A place where we become abstract and where we have the power to start over. 
Textile research comprehends three chapters from the drought to the oasis. 
<br>
Chapter two is inspired by Moroccan gear and architecture. The machine-knitted linen dress shows its fragility through hand-manipulated lace stitches.
There is a contrast in texture on the collar and cuffs by brushed-on alginate coating. The colour of the material derives from a red onion peel dye bath.
