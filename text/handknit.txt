Developed from material experimentations the full knit collection deals with the opposition to socially constructed gender norms.
The crochet is fully recycled either made out of old newspaper or raffia. All yarns are hand dyed in natural dye bath of beetroot, blueberrys and iron.
01: The paper crochet is created by using old newspaper articles from the “ZEIT” newspaper and combined with hand-knit pieces through an elongated handstitch, creating a symbiotic connection between the two.

02: The 2x1 rib knit fabric is dyed using beetroot waste and avocado skin and pits, resulting in unique and natural colors. The waistband is attached separately and dyed using blueberries and baking soda, adding another element of color to the garment.
