<br>
The dress is knitted on a single bed from cotton fibers. It features an overpanel made of linen that resembles delicate lace, elegantly entangling around both the body and the dress. The linen fabric is thoughtfully dyed using brown coffee grounds, symbolizing the significance of roots and the interconnected mycelium beneath the ground.
