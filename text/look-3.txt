Single bed, size 3, draped, linen, 10g glycerin, 24g alginate, 400ml natural dye bath.  
The dress, knitted on a single bed, is draped inside out using stitch size 3. An overpanel made of linen, resembling lace, entangles around the body and the dress itself. The linen fabric is dyed using brown coffee grounds, symbolizing the roots and the interconnected mycelium beneath the ground.
